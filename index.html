Architectural Finishes

Surface profiles.

We provide three distinct concrete finishes, tailored to the specific functional and aesthetic demands of your project.

Light Broom Finish

The industry standard for outdoor flatwork. A calculated micro-texture that ensures critical slip resistance in Atlantic weather, cleanly pulled for a modern, uniform aesthetic.

Smooth / Power Trowel

A highly refined, glass-like surface. Primarily executed on garage slabs, indoor flooring, and structural pads. It eliminates dusting and is incredibly simple to sweep or wash.

Exposed Aggregate

A premium decorative application. We strip away the top matrix of cement to reveal the natural, embedded stone beneath. It offers immense durability and a high-end textured look.

METHODOLOGY
Methodology

The A2Z Standard.

We don't guess. We follow a strict, proven methodology on every site to ensure structural integrity and a perfect finish.

01

Consultation

We review your site constraints, discuss load requirements, and provide a transparent, line-item estimate.

02

Prep & Forming

The foundation of the foundation. We excavate, establish drainage, compact the sub-base, and build rigid forms.

03

The Pour

Executing the pour under optimal conditions. We manage the mix, vibrate for density, and screed for perfect leveling.

04

Finish & Cure

We apply your chosen finish, cut deep control joints to manage expansion, and enforce a strict curing protocol.

About A2Z

We are not a volume builder. That's the point.

A2Z Creteworks is a specialized concrete crew operating out of Nova Scotia. We deliberately limit our project intake to ensure the owners are on-site, holding the trowel, and overseeing the pour on every single job.

When you hire a massive firm, you're paying for their salespeople and their overhead. When you hire us, you are communicating directly with the craftsmen building your driveway.

Structural Integrity

We refuse to pour thin slabs. We use a higher MPA mix and tighter rebar spacing than code requires.

Transparent Pricing

Our pricing is honest. We detail the material costs versus labor, so you understand exactly what you are investing in.

Immaculate Sites

Construction is chaotic; your property shouldn't be. We tarp adjacent areas and perform a surgical cleanup daily.
